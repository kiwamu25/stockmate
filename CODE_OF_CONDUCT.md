# Code of Conduct

This project expects all contributors and participants to keep discussions respectful, inclusive, and constructive.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and professional in all interactions.
- Assume good intent and focus on technical outcomes.
- Give and receive feedback in a constructive way.
- Keep discussions relevant to the project and its users.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, threats, or personal attacks.
- Abusive language, trolling, or repeated disruptive behavior.
- Sharing private or sensitive information without permission.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close discussions, or block contributors whose behavior violates this policy.

For reports, contact maintainers through a private channel when possible (for example, repository security advisory or direct maintainer contact).
